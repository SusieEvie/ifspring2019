Pebble Beach is an unincorporated community on the Monterey Peninsula in Monterey County, California. In addition to lying at sea level and being a small coastal residential community of mostly single-family homes, Pebble Beach is a resort destination and home to the famous golf courses of Cypress Point Club, Monterey Peninsula Country Club, and Pebble Beach Golf Links.
